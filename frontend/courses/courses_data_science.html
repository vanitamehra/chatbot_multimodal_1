Data Science Bootcamp

Duration: 12 weeks

Curriculum:

  • Week 1-2: Python Programming and Data Analysis
  • Week 3-4: Statistics and Probability
  • Week 5-6: Data Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
  • Week 7-8: Machine Learning Basics (Regression, Classification)
  • Week 9-10: Advanced ML (Clustering, NLP, Deep Learning)
  • Week 11: Capstone Project
  • Week 12: Deployment and Presentation

Admissions:

  • Application deadlines: Jan 31, May 31, Sep 30
  • Required documents: ID proof, transcript, resume, recommendation letter
  • Admission process:
    1. Fill online application form
    2. Interview with the admissions team
    3. Payment for the selected course
    4. Receive confirmation email
  • Scholarships: Available for meritorious students
